The opportunity: regulated invoice RWAs on XRPL

XRPL's Multi-Purpose Tokens (MPT) and lending primitives turn invoices into programmable real-world assets that can be structured to fit MiCA and similar regulatory frameworks.

Native RWA standard

MPTs live directly on the XRPL ledger with rich metadata, issuer controls and clawback. You get an on-ledger invoice registry instead of a database that merely claims to back a token.

Built for European regulation

XRPL's issuer model, regulated stablecoins and authorization flags make it easier to map roles like issuer, custodian and lender to MiCA categories such as e-money and asset-referenced tokens.

Fiat in, fiat out

Suppliers and lenders see familiar bank transfers. Under the hood, XRPL vaults and stablecoins handle settlement, giving you on-chain transparency with a traditional finance UX.
